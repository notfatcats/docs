---
title: 'Staff Role Configuration'
description: 'The configuration section provides essential settings to tailor Sparks behavior within your server. Below are the available configuration options and their purposes'
---

```
/configure staff-role
```

Ensure that the commands used in your server are exclusively employed by users you trust! A brief overview of each role configuration option is provided below:

- Moderator: This role empowers members to manage the points of anyone in the server.
- Administrator: Currently, this role holds no additional permissions. However, we recommend assigning it for potential future updates.
- Supervisory: Members with this role gain the ability to manage sessions.
- Management: This role grants the ability to configure certain modules and use basic commands.
- Ownership: Members with this role enjoy complete control over Spark's configurations.
